Game Basics
Scroll down to see full text if not visisble.

It's suggested that you play this game in full screen.
Press f11 (or) for Mac press Shift+⌘+F to enter full screen.

 This is game is quite easy to play and its interface very easy to understand but still...

The left stats bar of the game shows your currently equiped armor, current level, current and max health, attack damage, and armor value.

Below the left bar you can see your experience to next level bar, username, total monsters killed count, and current money.

Next to the left stats bar you can find your game window. Here you can see the game story log and the combat log.

Below the game window you can see the options area where you will will find options that you can click for continuing the story or find options that will help you during combat sequences.

Next to the options boxes there are two icon.
One that looks like a flask is the potions icon. You consume a potion upon clicking the potions icon. One important note: potions are used even when you are on full health. Use potions wisely, until you get rich.
Below the potions icon is the save icon that helps you save the game.
